Privacy Policy for Advanced Text Finder

Last updated: [Current Date]

Data Collection

Advanced Text Finder does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data or information. The extension operates entirely within your browser and does not communicate with external servers.

Information We Do NOT Collect

  • Search queries or text you search for
  • Website URLs or browsing history
  • Personal information of any kind
  • Usage analytics or statistics
  • Cookies or tracking data

How the Extension Works

Advanced Text Finder processes web page content locally in your browser to find text matches and identify interactive elements. All processing happens on your device and no information leaves your browser.

Permissions Usage

  • activeTab: To access current page content for text searching
  • scripting: To inject search functionality into web pages
  • host permissions: To operate on all websites you visit
  • webNavigation: To maintain functionality across page navigation

Third-Party Services

This extension does not use any third-party services, analytics, or external APIs.

Changes to Privacy Policy

Any updates to this privacy policy will be reflected in the extension's listing on the Chrome Web Store.
